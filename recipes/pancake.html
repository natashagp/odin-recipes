Pancake

Description

The best and easiest pancake recipe.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups of wheat flour
  • 3 eggs
  • 2 cups of milk
  • pitch of salt

Steps

  1. Mix all ingredients in a blender for 2 minutes.
  2. Then turn it off and, with a spoon, mix the flour that stuck to the blender glass.
  3. Mix again just to mix and set aside.
  4. Grease the frying pan with a drizzle of oil and heat until hot.
  5. Using a ladle, take a portion of the dough and place it in the frying pan. Rotate the pan to spread the dough evenly.
  6. Lower the heat and let it brown underneath, then turn it over to the other side and let it brown, repeat the process with all the dough.
