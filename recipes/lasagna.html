Lasagna

Description

The best and easiest lasagna recipe.

Ingredients

  • Meat
  • Onion and garlic
  • Tomato Sauce
  • Spices and seasonings
  • Lasagna noodles
  • Mozzarella cheese
  • Parmesan cheese

Steps

  1. In a pan fry the onion and garlic.
  2. Cook the meat with spices and seasoning, and add the fried onion and garlic.
  3. Make the meat sauce by mixing the cooked meat with the tomato sauce.
  4. Cook the lasagna noodles.
  5. Layer the lasagna in this order:
    1. Meat sauce
    2. Lasagna noodles
    3. Mozzarella cheese
    4. Meat sauce
    5. Lasagna noodles
    6. Mozzarella cheese
    7. Parmesan cheese
  6. Cover with foil and bake for 30 minutes in 180oC.
  7. Let the lasagna rest before serving.
