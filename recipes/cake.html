Cake

Description

The best and easiest chocolate cake recipe.

Ingredients

  • 3 eggs
  • 2 cups of wheat flour
  • 1/2 cup of oil
  • pitch of salt
  • 1 1/2 cup of sugar
  • 1 cup of chocolate powder
  • 1 tbps of baking powder
  • 1 cup of hot water

Steps

  1. In a blender, mix the eggs, sugar, oil, chocolate powder and wheat flour.
  2. Pour the dough into a bowl and add the hot water and baking powder, mixing well.
  3. Pour the dough into a greased pan and bake in a preheated medium-high oven (200Â° C) for 40 minutes.
  4. Unmold while still hot.
  5. Let the cake rest before serving.
